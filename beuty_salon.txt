Services & Policies
Cancellations: Must cancel at least 24 hours in advance. Late cancellations incur a 50% fee.

Pets: Service animals only.
Service Details (For Context)
Balayage: Includes toner and gloss. Takes 3-4 hours. Do not wash hair just before.
Keratin Treatment: Semi-permanent smoothing. Lasts 3-5 months. Formaldehyde-free options available.
Extensions: Consultation required for first-time clients. We use Tape-ins and K-Tips.
